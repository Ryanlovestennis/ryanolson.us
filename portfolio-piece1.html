Shoji Lamp

Inspired by traditional Japanese woodworking ethos the idea behind this project was to avoid overly complicated design and excessive staining or finishing. This simplicity allows the construction and quality of materials to stand out creating a certain understated elegance. The sapele body is held together by mortise and tenon joinery that the pine cross lap frames snugly sit inside of. Nori paste, the only adhesive in the entire construction, binds the rice paper shades to the pine frames.
